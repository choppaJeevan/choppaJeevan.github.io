Jeevan Sai Reddy Choppa

I recently completed my Master of Science in Data Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. Prior to joining UMD, I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College.

I build LLM and machine learning systems end to end, owning everything from data curation to evaluation. I've built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline that answers questions over long research PDFs using hybrid search and reranking, fine-tuned Llama-3-8B into a data science code assistant on an instruction corpus I curated myself, and designed an agentic AutoML engine that takes a raw CSV and autonomously profiles it, preprocesses it, and selects the best model.

What ties these together is an interest in retrieval, efficient fine-tuning, and agentic systems — and a conviction that the unglamorous parts (leakage guards, held-out evaluation, sandboxing generated code) are what separate a demo from a system you can trust.
