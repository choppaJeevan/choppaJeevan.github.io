About Me

I recently completed my Master of Science in Data Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. Prior to joining UMD, I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College.

My work focuses on building end-to-end machine learning and large language model systems. I am particularly interested in retrieval-augmented generation, efficient fine-tuning of LLMs, and agentic systems — and in the engineering that makes them practical at scale: hybrid search and retrieval infrastructure, rigorous evaluation, and reliable production pipelines.
