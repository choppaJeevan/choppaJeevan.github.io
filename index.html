Jeevan Sai Reddy Choppa

I hold a Master's in Data Science from the University of Maryland, College Park, and I want to build my career in Machine Learning and AI engineering. When my work mode is switched off, you'll find me on the tennis or badminton court, racing wheel-to-wheel in F1 25, or patiently building my little town in Township.

My work is fueled by a love for problem-solving, and I'm passionate about building AI systems that solve real-world problems end to end. I'm fascinated by how large language models work and what it takes to make them genuinely useful: teaching them to find the right information, adapt to new domains, and act on their own. I thrive on owning a problem from raw data to working system, and I believe the unglamorous parts, like careful evaluation and guarding against subtle mistakes, are what separate a demo from a system you can trust.

If you're building something interesting with AI, or just want to argue about racing in F1, my inbox is always open.
